Webcam Synth

The webcam synth is made possible by ml5.js handpose technology maintained by NYU's ITP Program, and p5.js was used for creating this software. In this software, the handpose technology detects a hand by drawing multiple ellipses around one hand and its fingers.

I connected these ellipses to certain number values on the x and y axis, and then I used tone.js library to trigger these number values as frequencies.

By setting certain parameters, I can change this software to any 7-note scale in any musical range.

Even though this is a simple prototype, I imagine a world where people can create a whole spectrum of sounds and trigger instruments with their body movements.
